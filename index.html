Bio

I'm a Game Design and Development Major, and so I obviously like video games! I'm also interested in music, and I love jazz; I play the saxophone in the jazz ensemble here. My overall goal is to end up developing games with the same amount of detail and charm of the games that I grew up, like Mario 64 and Ocarina of Time. I'd like to get a working knowledge of the web out of this class. Specifically, I'd like to understand more about server-side programming and how to use web services. At RIT, I'd like to continue to develop a strong social life and a strong educational knowledge of software development and music. Finally, when I graduate, I'd either like to be working for a AAA game studio or be running my own game development company.
